What is Keyword Extractor?

The Keyword Extractor Chrome extension allows you to easily extract and compile relevant keywords from any webpage with just a single click. Perfect for students, researchers, and content creators!

Screenshots

How It Works

1. Install the Keyword Extractor extension from the Chrome Web Store.
2. Navigate to any webpage you want to extract keywords from.
3. Click the Keyword Extractor icon in your toolbar.
4. Press the "Extract Keywords" button, and watch the magic happen!
5. Copy your keywords with a click and paste them wherever you need.
