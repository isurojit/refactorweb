Privacy Policy for Keyword Extractor

Effective Date: October 25, 2024

Introduction

This Privacy Policy outlines the types of personal information that is received and collected by the Keyword Extractor Chrome extension and how it is used.

Information We Collect

The Keyword Extractor extension does not collect any personal data from its users. It only processes data related to the webpage currently open in the browser to extract relevant keywords.

How We Use Your Information

The extension uses the information extracted from the currently active tab to provide keyword extraction services. This information is processed locally and is not sent to any external servers.

Permissions

The extension requests the following permissions:

  • activeTab: Allows the extension to access the content of the currently active tab for keyword extraction.
  • scripting: Enables the execution of scripts on the active tab to perform the keyword extraction.

Third-Party Services

The extension integrates a PayPal donation button that allows users to support the developer. This button is linked to PayPal's services, which may collect personal information in accordance with their own privacy policy.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
